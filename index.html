Two players share an unbiased coin and take it in turns to play "The Race".

On Player 1's turn, they toss the coin once: if it comes up Heads, they score one point; if it comes up Tails, they score nothing.

On Player 2's turn, they chooses a positive integer T and toss the coin T times: if it comes up all Heads, they score 2^(T-1) points; otherwise, they score nothing.

Player 1 goes first. The winner is the first to 100 or more points.
